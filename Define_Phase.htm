Define Phase Components

Inputs

–Project Charter
 

Activity

–Define the project’s purpose and scope
–Gather background on the process and the Customer's
 

Deliverable

–A high level map of the process
–A translation of the “Voice of the Customer” into “Key Quality Characteristics”
–Link projects to corporate objectives
–Financial impact
