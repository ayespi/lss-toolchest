Value Stream Process Flow Charts: The value-added flow chart is a mechanism to improve cycle times and productivity by visually separating value-adding from non-value-adding activities

Lean 5S Tool: 5S is the Japanese concept for House Keeping.

1.) Sort (Seiri)
2.) Straighten (Seiton)
3.) Shine (Seiso)
4.) Standardize (Seiketsu)
5.) Sustain (Shitsuke)
